👋 Hey Tester! Read this first.
You are helping make this website better for kids your age! Your job is to try everything on the website and tell us what works great and what feels confusing or broken. There are no wrong answers — if something feels weird or doesn't make sense to you, that is VERY useful feedback!

How to use this sheet:
1. Go through each test one by one.
2. Follow the steps in the "What To Do" column.
3. Check if what happened matches the "Should See" column.
4. Tick ✅ PASS or mark ❌ FAIL in the last column.
5. Write any extra notes in the "Your Notes" area at the end of each section.

Ask a grown-up for help if you get stuck — that is also important feedback!
